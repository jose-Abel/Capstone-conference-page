Blockchain Conferences Global Summit 2020

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Blockchain Conferences affiliate network in a different country every two years.

This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Blockchain, free culture and open knowledge.

So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates. Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant conferences together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about BC Global Summit 2020!
